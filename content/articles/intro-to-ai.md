# Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions.

## Types of AI

1. **Narrow AI**: Designed for specific tasks (e.g., facial recognition, search engines).
2. **General AI**: A machine that could successfully perform any intellectual task that a human being can. (Currently theoretical).
3. **Super AI**: Exceeds human intelligence.
